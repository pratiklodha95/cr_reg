ABOUT

Mood Indigo, IIT Bombay's cultural festival has grown to become Asia's largest college cultural festival. Over the years, it has seen a consistent increase in the footfall and college participation with 1,26,000 students visiting the festival from over 1600 colleges from all over the world in Mood Indigo 2014.

Mood Indigo's CR system plays an integral part in its growth and serves as a major platform for distribution of information. It helps Mood Indigo in providing opportunities to the talented students from colleges all over the country and promoting youth talent. College Representatives will be a key element in fulfilling this vision in Mood Indigo 2015.

There will be some explanation about Mood Indigo and the CR system. CR system is just a way to reach out to maximum crowd all over the country.

RESPONSIBILITIES OF A CR

1. Social Media:
Increasing outreach by sharing posts in relevant college groups.
2. Putting up posters of Mood Indigo on college notice boards.
3. Coordinating with Mood Indigo team in organising multicity events.
